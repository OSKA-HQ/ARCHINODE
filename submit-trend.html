Editorial Guidelines

01

Inspiration over advertisement

Tell the story behind a collection, the materials, the design philosophy. Avoid 'our product is the best' framing — Korean readers respond to context and craft.

02

Original content only

No press release reprints. Submit content created specifically for the Korean audience or freshly translated from your editorial team. Image rights must be cleared.

03

Korean translation provided

Submit in English or Italian. ARCHINODE editorial provides Korean translation as part of the review process. You may also submit your own Korean version for editor verification.
