Why Korea

A Market You Can't Ignore

South Korea is the world's fastest-growing premium design market. With a consumer base that adopts European design trends in 1–2 years (vs. 3–5 years elsewhere in Asia), Korea offers unmatched opportunities for architecture and design brands.

Rapid Trend Adoption

Korean consumers are early adopters of global design trends. Trends that take 3–5 years to spread in Europe reach mainstream Korea in just 1–2 years.

Renovation Boom

Millions of apartments built in the 1980s–90s are entering renovation cycles, driving massive demand for premium finishes, fixtures, and furniture.

Design-Savvy Consumers

Korean millennials and Gen Z invest heavily in their living spaces. Interior design is a cultural priority, not just a luxury.

Gateway to Asia

Success in Korea validates your brand across Asia. Korean taste is a benchmark for China, Japan, and Southeast Asian markets.

Professional Network

Korea has over 50,000 registered architects and 30,000+ interior design firms actively specifying international products.

Premium Willingness

Korean consumers are willing to pay premium prices for European quality. The "Made in Italy" and "Made in Germany" labels carry strong brand equity.
