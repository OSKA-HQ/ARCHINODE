Dealership Screening

When a Korean company expresses interest in becoming your dealer, we investigate and verify them on your behalf. Online screening from $100, in-person meeting and detailed report from $1,000 — far less than a flight to Korea.

After you've established a dealership, we continuously monitor your Korean dealer to ensure they're upholding brand guidelines, meeting commitments, and operating with integrity.

Exhibition Support

Want to exhibit at a Korean trade show? We handle everything — venue booking, booth design and setup, interpretation services, logistics, and on-site support throughout the event.
